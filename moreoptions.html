ATM Instructions

Welcome to our ATM service! Follow the instructions below for a smooth transaction:

  1. Insert your ATM card into the card reader with the chip facing up.
  2. Enter your PIN using the keypad provided.
  3. Select your desired transaction (e.g., Withdraw, Deposit, Balance Inquiry).
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the transaction.
  5. Remember to take your card and transaction receipt before leaving the ATM.

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact our customer support.
